Welcome to the Capacitor Museum



Probably the world's largest collection of electrolytic capacitors from unusual (and usual) brands!
If you have information about a particular capacitor or want to donate something not shown here, please use the contact form at the bottom of nixlabs.com.au. Generally only small electronics-type electrolytic capacitors are collected unless it is particularly unusual or interesting.
