Welcome to the Capacitor Museum



Probably the world's largest collection of electrolytic capacitors from unusual (and usual) brands!
If you have information about a particular capacitor or want to donate something not shown here, please use the contact form at the bottom of nixlabs.com.au. Generally only electronics-type through-hole electrolytic capacitors are collected unless it is particularly unusual or interesting.
Many thanks to capacitor.web.fc2.com as an invaluable resource for deciphering some of the markings.
